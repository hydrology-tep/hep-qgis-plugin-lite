Temporal resolution

Applies for reach output only.
Compute figures and performance statistics for discharge data on daily, weekly or monthly time scale.
Daily and weekly statistics are computed based on daily model and observation data (IPRINT = 1 in file.cio).
Monthly time scale applies to monthly model and observation data only (IPRINT = 0 in file.cio).
